# Founderflow: Project Participation Agreement

**This Participation Agreement** ("Agreement") is dated as of [Date], by and between **Founderflow** ("Provider") and the undersigned participant ("Participant").

### 1. The Founderflow Engine
Founderflow provides an autonomous outreach engine designed to run locally on the Participant's hardware. The engine facilitates lead engagement via Instagram based on lead lists provided by the Participant.

### 2. Operation & Access
- **Local Execution:** The software runs on the Participant’s local device. Founderflow does not host your account or store your passwords.
- **Session Capture:** Access is granted via local session token capture (cookies). Participant is never required to share their login password with Provider.

### 3. Compliance & Safety
- **Platform Integrity:** Provider has implemented "Human-Mimic" delay protocols to protect the Participant's account reputation.
- **Responsibility:** Participant acknowledges that automated outreach carries inherent risks on third-party platforms. Founderflow is not liable for any account restrictions or actions taken by Instagram.

### 4. Data Privacy
All lead data, session tokens, and outreach history remain private to the Participant's local device. Founderflow does not harvest or transmit this data to external servers.

### 5. Terms of the Project
This is a project-based pilot. Either party may end the relationship at any time.
