Under the Hood

How the engine works

A technical look at the three systems that power FounderFlow — from lead discovery to AI-powered call booking.

01. Lead Discovery Engine

The discovery engine searches Instagram and other platforms for potential clients in your niche. It uses Instagram's search UI — not API scraping — to find accounts that match your target profile. Every lead is checked for relevance before being added to the pool.

The engine mimics human behavior: warming up the session, browsing between searches, and spacing out requests to avoid detection. No rapid-fire API calls.

UI-based search Human-like pacing Niche filtering Deduplication

02. DM Automation & Follow-ups

The outreach engine sends personalized DMs to discovered leads at a safe pace — 50-75 per day, with randomized delays between each message. It uses a real browser session (not an API bot), so every DM looks like it comes from a real person.

If a lead doesn't reply within 48 hours, a follow-up message goes out automatically. Up to two follow-ups per lead, then they're marked as inactive. No spam, no harassment.

50-75 DMs/day Randomized delays Auto follow-ups Action block detection

03. AI Setter (Auto-Responder)

When a lead replies, the AI Setter takes over. Powered by Gemini, it reads the incoming message, generates a natural reply in your voice, and sends it — all within seconds. No canned responses, no templates. Every reply is generated fresh based on the conversation context.

The AI is trained on your persona, your product, and common objections. It knows when to share more info, when to ask qualifying questions, and when to send your Calendly link. It also detects intent — mapping each conversation to a pipeline status (replied, meeting booked, not interested, etc.).

Gemini-powered Persona-trained Intent detection Calendly integration Objection handling

04. CRM Pipeline

Every lead is tracked through a clear pipeline: Discovered → DM Sent → Replied → Meeting Booked → Qualified → Closed. You can see exactly where every lead stands, filter by status, and update stages manually or let the AI do it automatically.

The dashboard shows real-time stats: leads discovered, DMs sent, reply rates, calls booked. You can also see the full DM history for any lead — every message sent and received, all in one place.

Real-time pipeline DM history Status tracking Analytics

05. Account Safety

FounderFlow runs on your own Instagram account using a real browser session — not a bot farm. The engine includes multiple safety layers: human-like typing speed, randomized delays, warmup mode for new accounts, and action block detection that pauses outreach immediately if Instagram flags anything.

Warmup mode starts at 30% of your daily limit and ramps up over 14 days, so new accounts are protected from day one.

Real browser session Warmup mode Action block detection Human-like typing Crash protection
