01. Discovery (The Claw)

The Claw utilizes deep-web crawling and social metadata extraction to identify prospects. It doesn't just "scrape"—it validates MMR (Monthly Recurring Revenue) and follower growth trends to ensure every lead is a "Whale."

The Hub is the matrix execution engine. It handles high-volume outreach across multiple platforms while utilizing the Humanizer Protocol—a system of random jitter, varied delay ranges, and unique hardware thumbprints to keep your social accounts 100% safe.

The Brain is the neural closing layer. Using the Gemini 1.5 Pro API, it monitors conversation threads for text-only intent. It automatically filters out media noise (Reels/Memes) and provides expert context-aware replies to book meetings on autopilot.
